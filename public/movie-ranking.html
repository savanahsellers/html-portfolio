The Best Movies According to Savanah

My top three movies of all-time

The Boy and The Heron

Another masterpiece brought to us by Hayao Miyazaki. He never fails to impress; truly bringing art back to cinema.

Hot Fuzz

A fun and quirky British comedy that is always a pleasure to watch. This is my favorite Christmas movie.

The Princess Bride.

Inconceivable! My lifetime favorite.
